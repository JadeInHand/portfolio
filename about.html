    • In my own time since the course has ended I have been teaching myself React, Node.js, Mongoose and numerous other technologies. If you have any suggestions for other techonologies, don't hesitate to shoot me a message

      • Achievements in Code:


      SydStart Hackathon 2015
    • The week after finishing the course, myself and 3 other course mates decided to enter the SydStart 2015 Hackathon, where the challenge was to make a product using the Freelancer API. We had from 10am Thursday, too 2pm Friday to complete the challenge and then pitch.

    • Our teams idea that we put forward was "TeachLancer", a way for students to connect with teachers through the Freelancer API.

    • My team-mates and I were lucky enough to be selected as finalists in this Hackathon and won a cash prize of $1000, and more importantly, we got a large novelty check (checked it off the bucket list).

    • A Fantastic learning experience of how to deal with high pressue, high expectation situations and how to manage those issues and put out a quality product.
